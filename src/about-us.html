About us

Swoop is an online magazine for skydivers and people who are interested in skydiving. It provides information about skydiving, drop zones, events and competitions.

Swoop was founded in 2020 by Jason Stettler, who has been skydiving for eight years. Originally working as a web designer, he decided to combine his two passions and create an online magazine for skydivers.

Swoop is based in the UK, but has contributors from all over the world. If you are interested in contributing to Swoop, please contact us.
