About WaterAid

Our Mission and Impact

Welcome to WaterAid, where we're dedicated to transforming lives through access to clean water, sanitation, and hygiene. For over four decades, we've been at the forefront of the global effort to ensure that everyone, everywhere has access to these basic human rights.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to make clean water, decent toilets, and good hygiene normal for everyone, everywhere within a generation. We work tirelessly in some of the world's toughest places to reach the most marginalized communities, empowering them with sustainable solutions that last.

Through partnerships with local organizations, governments, and communities, we implement projects that not only provide immediate access to clean water and sanitation but also promote long-term behavior change and community ownership. With your support, we've been able to reach millions of people with life-changing services, but there's still much work to be done.

Join us in our mission to create a world where no one is left behind because of a lack of clean water or sanitation. Together, we can make a difference.
